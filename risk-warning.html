Risk Warning

Last updated: March 2026

This risk warning is provided by FX Spectrum Infotech Co., Ltd. ("FX Spectrum") to inform you of the potential risks associated with trading foreign exchange (forex), contracts for difference (CFDs), cryptocurrencies, and other financial instruments.

1. High Risk Investment

Trading forex, CFDs, and cryptocurrencies involves a high level of risk and may not be suitable for all investors. You could sustain a loss of some or all of your invested capital. You should not invest money that you cannot afford to lose.

2. Leverage Risk

Leveraged trading can amplify both profits and losses. A small adverse market movement can result in substantial losses that may exceed your initial deposit. Ensure you fully understand the implications of leverage before trading.

3. Market Volatility

Financial markets are subject to rapid and unpredictable price movements influenced by economic events, geopolitical developments, and other factors beyond your control. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

4. Expert Advisors (EA) & Automated Trading

While Expert Advisors and automated trading systems can assist in executing trading strategies, they carry inherent risks:

  • Technical failures, including internet connectivity issues and server downtime
  • Algorithm limitations in unusual or extreme market conditions
  • Past performance of any EA does not guarantee future profitability
  • Regular monitoring and adjustment is required

5. No Guarantee of Profit

FX Spectrum does not guarantee profits or protection against losses. Any market analysis, signals, tools, or educational content provided is for informational purposes only and should not be construed as personalized investment advice.

6. Independent Advice

Before making any investment decisions, we strongly recommend seeking independent financial, legal, and tax advice from qualified professionals who can assess your individual circumstances.

7. Regulatory Notice

Trading regulations vary by jurisdiction. It is your responsibility to ensure that your trading activities comply with the laws and regulations applicable in your country of residence.

8. Acknowledgment

By using FX Spectrum's services, tools, and educational materials, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted the risks described in this warning. You agree that FX Spectrum shall not be held liable for any losses arising from your trading activities.
